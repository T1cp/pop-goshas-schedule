                Task Syntax
            
      
            
      
                Adding tasks — Click + Add on any date in Calendar or Today.
      
                Priorities — High (red dot), Medium (amber), Low (grey).
      
                Repeating — Set repeat in Master Tasks for daily, weekly, monthly, or custom intervals.
      
                Due times — Click the clock icon to set a specific time with AM/PM.
            
      
        
      
        
      
            
      
                Ledger Management
            
      
            
      
                Income vs Expenses — Toggle the type when adding entries. Green = income, red = expense.
      
                Period navigation — Use the ‹ › arrows at the top to switch months.
      
                Categories — Assign categories to track spending patterns.
            
      
        
      
        
      
            
      
                Schedule Sharing
            
      
            
      
                Adding friends — Go to Social & Chat, type their username in "Add by username…" and hit send.
      
                Granting access — Under Schedule Sharing, enter a friend's username. They must accept before viewing.
      
                View-only mode — When viewing a friend's schedule, a banner appears. Edits are blocked.
            
      
        
      
        
      
            
      
                Direct Messages & Groups
            
      
            
      
                Open a DM — Click any friend in the Social & Chat tab to open a direct message.
      
                Create a group — Click "New Group Chat", name the group, pick friends, hit Create.
      
                Reactions — Right-click (or long-press) any message to react with an emoji.
            
      
        
      
        
      
            
      
                Customisation
            
      
            
      
                Accent colours — Click the ⚙ icon in the top bar to open Settings. Colour swatches are at the top.
      
                Location & weather — Set your city in Settings for accurate weather on the Today view.
      
                Tab visibility — Toggle which tabs appear in the nav from the Settings panel.
